Course¶

  • In my notes, I used Patrick M. Wensing’s course Optimization-Based Robotics to create the outline. In addition, I embed his course notes into my notes.

    • You can get the course notes from the website.

    • Use an e-mail and ask Professor Wensing for the recording of previous lectures.

  • A related, more control-focused course is Russ Tedrake’s Underactuated Robotics.

    • There is an online book.

    • The lectures are available on YouTube.

I am grateful to both for their amazing work.
